Beyond Elo

Innovative signals in the model

FM

Decayed form

Recent results are weighted by an exponential half-life, so a team's last month matters more than its last year — without a hard cutoff.

KO

Knockout pressure

Elimination matches get a scoring discount, reflecting the historically more cautious, lower-scoring tactics teams use once a loss ends their tournament.

H2H

Bogey-team factor

Average deviation from Elo expectation across each pair's last 5 meetings — captures rivalries Elo alone can't explain.

RST

Fatigue proxy

Days since each team's last match, surfacing squad freshness heading into a congested knockout schedule.

CF

Confederation prior

For thin-history debutants, the model backs off to their confederation's average Elo rather than a flat global prior.

MC

Bracket simulation

Scorelines are sampled from Poisson rates and carried through the full 48-team bracket thousands of times for true title odds.
